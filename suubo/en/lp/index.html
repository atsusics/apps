Suubo

Vacuum the world into the stars

Features

One hand. Eight worlds. Real scale.

Suck up the world around you and grow. From your bedroom to the multiverse — all in 3 minutes.

🕹️

One-handed Floating Joystick

Play with one thumb, anywhere. Press anywhere on the screen to spawn the floating joystick and steer Suubo with ease.

🌍

Real-scale Journey

Paperclip → chair → car → building → mountain → planet → galaxy. Every world is connected at true scale in one seamless universe.

🌌

8 Worlds

Room, garden, city, Japan, Earth, space, galaxy, multiverse. Suubo grows through every one of them as you vacuum up the cosmos.

⏱️

3-Minute Runs

Quick, sharp, replayable. A 3-minute timer keeps every session tight and satisfying — perfect for a spare moment.

How to Play

Four simple steps

Suck, grow, and reach for the stars.

1

Move with the joystick

Press anywhere on the screen to spawn the floating joystick. Steer Suubo around the world.

2

Suck up smaller things

Get close to anything smaller than Suubo and it gets absorbed automatically. Keep growing.

3

Move through worlds

When you've maxed out in your current world, the next one opens. Push through all 8.

4

Beat the clock

You have 3 minutes. How far into the universe can Suubo reach?
