Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Suubo ("the App") is an iOS application provided by Atsushi Oshima ("the Developer"). Please review this Privacy Policy before using the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect or transmit your personal information. Game progress data is stored only on your device and is never sent to the Developer's servers or any third party.

The App does not access your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or photos.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect information such as your advertising identifier (IDFA) and device information for ad optimization purposes.

On first launch, the App will display an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. Your advertising identifier will be used for ad personalization only if you grant permission. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown.

In the EEA, UK, and other applicable regions, the App uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect your consent for advertising.

For more information on how AdMob handles data, please review Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Policy

Third-Party Data Sharing

The Developer does not sell or share your data with any third parties. Information transmitted to Google AdMob for advertising purposes is described in the "Advertising" section above.

In-App Purchases

The App does not contain any in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

The App is available for use by anyone regardless of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
