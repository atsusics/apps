Features

Don't let garlic ruin your next plan

Just log what you ate. The app tells you when the smell will be gone.

🧄

One-tap Logging

Pick cloves for raw garlic or spoons for cooked. Logging takes just a few taps.

📊

Odor Score

A real-time number from your intake and elapsed time. Danger levels trigger an alert.

⏱️

Time-to-Clear

Auto-calculated hours until you're odorless. Know at a glance if you'll make it to your next meeting.

🥛

Countermeasure Tips

Milk, apple juice, green tea, tooth brushing. The app recommends the right fix for your current level.

📅

Calendar History

Look back on your garlic life in a calendar view. Every intake, one tap away.

🔒

Fully On-device

All data lives on your phone. No account, no server, no tracking of your eating habits.

How to Use

Four simple steps

Eat, log, and let the app do the rest.

1

Eat garlic

Dumplings, ramen, aglio e olio — enjoy today's garlic meal.

2

Log the amount

Pick the number of cloves or spoons. A few taps and you're done.

3

Check your score

See your current odor score and the time you'll be clear.

4

Take action

If you're in the danger zone, follow the recommended countermeasure.
