Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Garlic Breath Meter ("the App") is an iOS application provided by Atsushi Oshima ("the Developer"). Please review this Privacy Policy before using the App.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect or transmit any personal information. All data, including garlic intake records (date, amount, and notes), is stored solely on your device and is never sent to external servers by the Developer.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect the advertising identifier (IDFA), device information, and approximate location in order to optimize ad delivery.

On first launch the App presents an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent dialog. If you do not grant permission, only non-personalized ads will be served.

For more information on how AdMob handles data, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads Policies

Third-Party Data Sharing

The Developer does not sell or share your data with any third parties. Information sent to Google AdMob for the purpose of ad delivery is described in the "Advertising" section above.

In-App Purchases

The App does not contain any in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

The App is available for use by anyone regardless of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
